MQT_BuySellPressure

Instant Buy/Sell Pressure — Smoothed, Filtered, and Flashing When It Matters

MQT_BuySellPressure estimates buy vs sell volume pressure using each bar’s OHLC structure and volume, smooths the result with configurable EMAs, and displays a clean “Buy Pressure” or “Sell Pressure” readout on your chart when one side dominates above 50%. Optional pulse/flash behavior highlights extreme pressure conditions so you can spot momentum shifts faster.

Key Features

Dominant-Side Display (Above 50%)

Only one line is displayed at a time: Buy Pressure when buy > 50%, or Sell Pressure when sell > 50%. If neither side dominates, nothing is drawn.

Smoothed Pressure via EMA

Uses EMA smoothing on estimated buy and sell volumes with independent lengths: BuyVolumeAvgLength and SellVolumeAvgLength.

Flash/Pulse on Extreme Pressure

Enable flashing when pressure exceeds FlashThresholdPercent, with adjustable speed via FlashIntervalMs.

Movable Overlay Panel

Drag-and-drop the display anywhere on your chart. Configure sizing and placement with PanelWidth, PanelHeight, PanelX, and PanelY.

Right-Anchored Placement Option

Use UseRightAnchor to interpret PanelX as distance from the right edge, perfect for consistent top-right positioning.

Typography + Shadow Controls

Choose FontWeightStyle, FontSizePx, and optional text shadow for better contrast: EnableShadow, ShadowOpacity, ShadowOffsetX, ShadowOffsetY.

How It Works

1

Estimate Buy vs Sell Volume

Each bar’s OHLC structure is used to estimate “raw” buy and sell volume portions, scaled by the bar’s total volume.

2

Smooth the Readings

Buy and sell series are smoothed using EMAs with independent lengths to reduce noise and stabilize the percentage.

3

Convert to Percent Pressure

Buy% and Sell% are computed from the smoothed totals so the two sides always sum to ~100%.

4

Display Only the Dominant Side

If Buy% > 50%, show Buy Pressure. If Sell% > 50%, show Sell Pressure. Optional flashing highlights extremes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I only see Buy Pressure or Sell Pressure (not both)?

By design, the indicator draws only the dominant side when its pressure is above 50%. If neither side is above 50%, it draws nothing to avoid indecision clutter.

Does this use bid/ask or order flow data?

No. It estimates buy/sell pressure from OHLC positioning within the bar and the bar’s total volume, then smooths the series using EMAs.

How do I make the pressure less “jumpy”?

Increase BuyVolumeAvgLength and SellVolumeAvgLength to smooth the values more. You can also slow down flashing by increasing FlashIntervalMs.

How do I make the flash trigger only on extreme readings?

Raise FlashThresholdPercent (for example, 60, 70, or higher) so pulsing only occurs when pressure is strong.

Can I move the display?

Yes. Click and drag the text panel on the chart. You can also control position via PanelX, PanelY and enable right-side anchoring with UseRightAnchor.

What are Start/End time parameters used for?

They are available as settings in this version, but the current code does not apply a time-window filter in its calculations/rendering. If you want time-window filtering added, tell me your exact desired behavior (RTH only, custom window, etc.).

What is PressureThreshold used for?

It is exposed as a parameter, but the current render logic uses a fixed dominance threshold (> 50%) and the flash threshold. If you’d like PressureThreshold to control display/alerts, I can suggest a clean way to wire it in.
